About CustomMobs
CustomMobs is a powerful and easy-to-use Minecraft plugin that allows server owners to create fully customized mobs and bosses — all through a simple in-game GUI. No need to edit complex configuration files or to use external tools, as everything is handled directly inside Minecraft. With CustomMobs, you can easily:
  • Equip mobs with customizable weapons and armor. Any item you can create in-game, you can equip!
  • Define the name with colors (even Hex!) and mob type of the mobs.
  • Adjust the entity behavior and attributes like speed, health and aggressivity of animals.
  • Configure drop tables, specifying what items mobs will drop on death—and at what rates.
  • Modify spawner settings such as spawn range, cooldowns, and spawn limits.
  • Add special abilities or timed events, like casting spells, triggering explosion, or summoning allies.
  • Apply potion effects and sound effects on spawn or death of the mob.
  • Set personalized messages that appear when a mob spawns or dies.
    • Whether you're designing dungeon bosses, story-based encounters, or just want to populate your world with more dynamic creatures, CustomMobs gives you full creative control. Once created, mobs can be spawned using custom eggs, commands, or through fully configurable mob spawners. With a user-friendly interface and deep customization options, CustomMobs makes mob creation fun, fast, and flexible.
